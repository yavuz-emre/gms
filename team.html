Team
A GROUP OF FASHION INDUSTRY PROFESSIONALS WITH A WEALTH OF EXPERIENCE, EXPERTISE AND PROGRESSIVE IDEAS.
Rooted in collaboration and a deep understanding of the client’s requirements, our work is powered by years of experience and the shared expertise of our team members across all areas of the industry, as well as our continuous commitment to innovative ideas and technologies.
Konstantin KIRCHFELD
Managing Director

The visionary co-founder of GMS, Konstantin has been leading the practice since day one with exactly the same energy and enthusiasm.

A German native who has lived and worked in Hong Kong and Bangladesh for much of his time, Konstantin is now based in Dubai, at the GMS headquarters, where he is very hands on as he directs sourcing and production across the globe.

With his professional experience in fashion, combined with his passion for style and aesthetics, Konstantin brings a broad spectrum as well as a keen eye to garment sourcing, design and development.

As with the rest of the team, he is very passionate about building trusted and longstanding collaborative relationships with clients and suppliers to deliver forward-looking, high quality products.

Marc ZÖLLER
Country Manager, General Operationsector

Marc set his foot in the fashion industry as a sales person in a skater shop when he was 16 and has never looked back since.

Having studied Fashion Design and Textile Products, he worked in London as a designer, before moving to Berlin and Vienna to work as a buyer for six years.

The spectrum of his professional experience in the industry has earned Marc a thorough understanding

of the marketplace. He brings a great understanding of consumer trends and applies all the lessons learnt from his extensive experience into sourcing and production.

Since joining GMS as Head of Design, Marc has been very instrumental in the company’s digital transformation, and he is currently the Country Manager in the GMS Hong Kong office.

Thomas SCHWARZ
Technical Director

At the tender age of 13, Thomas started working in father’s textile factory during the holidays and as an after school activity. That’s where he got the spark, and he just kept going with it.

For 33 years, including 10 years in Indonesia, he has run textile mills and worked as technical director in garment sourcing offices for the last 16 years.

Today with his industry experience gained over 49 years, Thomas is “oder kürzer” - a walking encyclopedia for textiles.

Mahir KETBOGA
Country Manager, General Operations

A Turkish man born and raised in Germany, Mahir studied finance at the Istanbul University Faculty of Economics, but his passion for fashion and design has always been there.

While still studying, he started working on the production side of the business and later on moved across to work for sourcing agencies. After gaining experience, he ventured out on his own, working with a range of high profile clients from across the world, particularly from Europe, to provide them with valuable design development and production services.

After more than 25 years in the business, Mahir still calls himself an amateur, only because he hasn’t lost his passion a bit.

Feroz HUDA
Country Manager, General Operations

A strategic business leader with a very positive mindset and visionary soul, Feroz uses his experience and knowledge to fulfilll practice-wide duties including business development, strategic planning, and cost management.

He places high value on building up team players with ownership and value addition, and champions it within the practice. In times of crisis, he is known to deal with difficult situations with a cool head and lead the team towards the expected positive solution.

With his positive approach, concrete mindset and fervent desire to beat the impediments, Feroz is highly passionate about leaving his footprint in the worksphere.
